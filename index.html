Phyo Si Thu

Born in April 1994 in Yangon City, Myanmar, I graduated from Myanmar Maritime University in 2016 with a degree in Marine Engineering. Following graduation, I embarked on a career as a marine engineer, gaining valuable experience during a full contract period. Seeking new opportunities and a change in lifestyle, I dedicated myself to studying Japanese for a year. In July 2019, I transitioned to Japan where I joined the manufacturing sector in Fukushima as a full-time permanent staff member specializing in the production of Coil Winding Machines. Over the course of approximately 2.5 years, I relocated to Tokyo and assumed the role of Field Service Engineer. During this time, I leveraged my engineering background to develop and implement ladder programs for controlling and optimizing Winding Machines. Additionally, I pursued further education in IT at Chuo Computer and Communication College, expanding my skills and knowledge in this dynamic field.
